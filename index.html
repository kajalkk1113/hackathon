SAMOSA RECIPE

INGREDIENTS FOR SAMOSE

    For Dough:-
  • 1 cup refined flour
  • 1/2tsp carom seeds
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Chilled Water as required (Stiff dough)


    • For Filling:-
  • 2-3 tbsp oil
  • 4-5 medium potatoes, boiled
  • 1 tbsp ginger, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tsp crushed coriander
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ¼ tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • Black pepper powder to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ tsp dried mango powder (amchur powder)
  • Oil for frying

calories:120-150 calories.

CHHOLE BHATURE RECIPE

INGREDIENTS FOR CHHOLE BHATURE

    For Chhole:
  • 1 cup chickpeas (chhole) - soaked overnight
  • 2 onions - finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes - pureed/chopped
  • tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies
  • 2 tbsp oil/ghee
  • Spices

    For Bhature:
  • 2 cups maida (all-purpose flour)
  • 2 tbsp semolina (sooji/rava)
  • 2 tbsp curd
  • 1/2tsp baking powder
  • Salt as per taste
  • Water for kneading
  • Oil for deep frying

calories:400-500 calories.

GUJIYA RECIPE

INGREDIENTS FOR GUJIYA

    For Dough:-
  • maida-1 cup
  • Semolina (sooji)-2 tbsp
  • Ghee-2 tbsp (for dough and frying)
  • Water-as needed to knead the dough


    • For Filling:
  • Khoya-1 cup
  • Powdered sugar -1 cup (adjust to taste)
  • Grated coconut -1/4 cup
  • Cashews - 8-10 (chopped)
  • Almonds- 8-10 (chopped)
  • Raisins- 2 tbsp
  • Cardamom powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Saffron strands- a few (optional)
  • Ghee -1 tbsp (for roasting the filling)

calories:146 to 236 calories
